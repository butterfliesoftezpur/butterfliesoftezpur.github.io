ABOUT THE PROJECT

Many species of butterflies are found in Tezpur and they are mostly of the family Nymphalidae,Riodinidae,Pieridae etc.They belong to the order Lepidoptera and their genuses mostly comprises of Junonia,Elymnias,Catopsilia,Danaus,Delias,Euploe etc.

.

The main objective of this project is collection of butterfly species from every nook and corner of Tezpur town through field survey,construction of an online database and to determine the evolutionary relationship between all the species by phylogenetic analysis and tree constuction.

This project is carried out jointly by Amlanjyoti Sarma and Madhurjya Borah of Bsc Biotechnology 6th Semester,under the mentorship of Mr.Parag Nath,Asst.Professor,Department of Biotechnology,Kaliabor College.
